<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A-Scan</li><li>B-Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymetry</li><li>Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ophthalmic ultrasound systems encompass various types used in eye diagnostics. A-Scan provides precise measurements of eye structures, crucial for cataract surgery planning. B-Scan offers a two-dimensional image, aiding in the assessment of retinal and other eye conditions. Combined Scan integrates both A- and B-Scan capabilities for comprehensive analysis. Pachymetry measures corneal thickness, essential in glaucoma assessment. Lastly, the Ultrasound Bio Microscope (UBM) delivers high-resolution images of anterior segment structures, facilitating detailed evaluations of complex eye conditions.</p></p>
