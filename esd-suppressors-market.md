<p><ul><li>Diode Array</li><li>Polymer</li><li>TVS</li><li>Zener</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ESD suppressors market is categorized into four primary types: Diode Array, Polymer, TVS (Transient Voltage Suppressors), and Zener. Diode Arrays offer multi-channel protection against electrostatic discharges and voltage spikes. Polymer suppressors provide flexibility and robustness for sensitive devices. TVS diodes react quickly to transients, ensuring high-speed protection in electronic circuits. Zener diodes maintain voltage regulation while protecting against overvoltage. Each type serves distinct applications, enhancing reliability and performance in electronic devices by preventing damage from ESD events.</p></p>
