<p><p>The smart meter market is categorized into single-phase and three-phase smart meters, each catering to different energy consumption needs. Single-phase smart meters are typically used in residential settings, measuring lower power demands effectively. In contrast, three-phase smart meters are designed for commercial and industrial applications, handling higher power loads and providing detailed usage data. Both types enhance energy management, improve billing accuracy, and enable real-time monitoring, contributing to the modernization of energy distribution systems.</p></p>
