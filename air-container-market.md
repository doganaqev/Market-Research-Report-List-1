<p><strong>The Air Container Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lower Deck Container</li><li>Main Deck Container</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air container market is divided into two main types: lower deck containers and main deck containers. Lower deck containers are designed for use in the cargo hold of aircraft, primarily for smaller loads and specialized items. In contrast, main deck containers are utilized on the upper level, allowing for larger and heavier shipments. Each type caters to specific cargo requirements, with the main deck offering greater accessibility and volume, while lower deck containers optimize the use of limited space in the holds.</p></p>
