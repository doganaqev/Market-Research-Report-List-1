<p><p>The tennis ball market comprises three main types: high pressure, low pressure, and pressureless balls. High pressure balls are standard and provide superior bounce and speed, making them ideal for competitive play but losing pressure over time. Low pressure balls have a thicker rubber shell, offering a softer feel and greater control, suitable for training and recreational play. Pressureless balls retain their form and bounce longer, making them durable and ideal for practice, but they lack the responsiveness of pressure balls.</p></p>
