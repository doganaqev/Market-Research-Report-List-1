<p><ul><li>Open Gantry Robot</li><li>Closed Gantry Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gantry robot market is categorized into open and closed gantry robots. Open gantry robots feature a frame that allows for unrestricted access around the working area, making them ideal for scenarios requiring flexibility and wide-ranging operations. In contrast, closed gantry robots have a structured enclosure, providing enhanced stability and protection for intricate tasks, often in CNC machining or assembly lines. Each type addresses specific operational needs, with open gantry robots favoring versatility and closed models emphasizing precision and safety.</p></p>
