<p><p>The fire-resistant glass market includes several types, each designed to provide varying degrees of heat resistance and safety. Laminated glass features layers of glass bonded with an interlayer to prevent shattering. Ceramic glass withstands high temperatures and offers excellent thermal resistance, making it ideal for fire-rated applications. Tempered glass is heat-treated for increased strength and safety. Wired glass incorporates metal wires within its structure, providing additional protection against breakage and maintaining structural integrity during a fire.</p></p>
