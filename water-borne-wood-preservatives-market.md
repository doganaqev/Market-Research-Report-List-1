Chromated copper arsenate (CCA) combines copper, chromium, and arsenic for robust wood preservation, commonly applied in outdoor projects. Borates serve as a less toxic option, effective against fungal decay and insect infestation. Other market types may include various hybrid systems and bio-based solutions, expanding choices for wood treatment applications.</p></p>
