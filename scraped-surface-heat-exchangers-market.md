<p><strong>The Scraped Surface Heat Exchangers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating Tubular Scraped Surface Heat Exchangers</li><li>Rotating Tubular Scraped Surface Heat Exchangers</li><li>Rotating Plate Scraped Surface Heat Exchangers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scraped Surface Heat Exchangers (SSHEs) are essential in processes requiring efficient heat transfer, particularly for viscous materials. There are three main types: Reciprocating Tubular SSHEs, which utilize a reciprocating scraper mechanism to maintain heat transfer efficiency; Rotating Tubular SSHEs, featuring rotating scrapers that continuously agitate the surface, enhancing heat transfer; and Rotating Plate SSHEs, employing rotating plates that scrape the product while facilitating heat exchange. Each type caters to specific applications based on material characteristics and process requirements.</p></p>
