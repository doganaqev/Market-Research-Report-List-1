<p><p>The Dry Milk Powder Metal Detector market is segmented into two primary types: low frequency and high frequency detectors. Low frequency detectors are designed for products with high moisture content, effectively identifying large metallic contaminants while minimizing false alarms. In contrast, high frequency detectors are optimized for dry milk powder's fine particle composition, ensuring precise detection of smaller metal fragments. Each type caters to different processing environments, offering tailored solutions for maintaining product safety and quality in the dairy industry.</p></p>
