<p><ul><li>Hypertonic Laxative</li><li>Lubricating Laxative</li><li>Stimulant Laxative</li><li>Volumetric Laxative</li><li>Swelling Laxative</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laxative market comprises various types, each designed to relieve constipation through different mechanisms. Hypertonic laxatives draw water into the intestines, softening stool. Lubricating laxatives coat the bowel's surface, easing stool passage. Stimulant laxatives trigger intestinal contractions, promoting movement. Volumetric laxatives increase stool bulk, stimulating bowel function. Swelling laxatives absorb water, expanding in the gut to facilitate evacuation. Additionally, "others" encompass combinations or lesser-known types, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences in gastrointestinal health management.</p></p>
