<p><ul><li>Angle Valve</li><li>Z-inline Valve</li><li>Y-inline Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum poppet valves market includes three main types: angle valves, Z-inline valves, and Y-inline valves. Angle valves allow fluid flow at a 90-degree angle, optimizing space and flow efficiency. Z-inline valves maintain a linear flow path, minimizing turbulence and are often used in systems requiring low pressure drop. Y-inline valves enable smoother flow transitions, reducing noise and wear. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing performance in vacuum systems across various industries.</p></p>
