One-component adhesives are pre-mixed formulations that are ready for use and simplify the application process, offering convenience and consistency. In contrast, multi-component adhesives require mixing two or more components prior to application, allowing for tailored properties and enhanced performance. These products cater to various needs in electronic manufacturing, providing solutions that balance ease of use with specific performance requirements for different applications.</p></p>
