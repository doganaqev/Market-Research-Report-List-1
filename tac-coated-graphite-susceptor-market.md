<p><p>The TaC coated graphite susceptor market includes two primary types: disc and barrel susceptors. Disc susceptors are flat, circular components designed for uniform heating and are commonly used in applications like semiconductor processing. Barrel susceptors, on the other hand, have a cylindrical shape, providing efficient heating for larger substrates or materials. Both types leverage the thermal conductivity of graphite and the corrosion resistance of tantalum carbide (TaC), making them essential for high-temperature, high-performance environments in various industries.</p></p>
