<p><p>The pleated HVAC filter market is segmented based on the Minimum Efficiency Reporting Value (MERV), indicating the filter's air cleaning efficiency. Filters rated MERV 7 and below are basic, capturing larger particles. MERV 8 filters improve this slightly, while MERV 9 and 10 provide better filtration of allergens. MERV 11 and 12 filters effectively capture smaller particulates, and MERV 13 filters target finer allergens and pollutants. MERV 14 and above offer high efficiency, suitable for environments requiring superior air quality, such as hospitals.</p></p>
