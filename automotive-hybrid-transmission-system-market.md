<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Multi Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive hybrid transmission system market is categorized into two main types: single-stage and multi-stage. Single-stage hybrid transmissions include one gear set, simplifying design and enhancing efficiency in applications with limited power demands. In contrast, multi-stage hybrid transmissions feature multiple gear sets, allowing for greater flexibility and performance optimization across a broader range of operating conditions. This versatility makes multi-stage systems suitable for high-performance vehicles, while single-stage systems are often preferred for affordability and compactness in simpler applications.</p></p>
