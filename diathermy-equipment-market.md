<p><p>The diathermy equipment market includes three primary types: microwave diathermy, shortwave diathermy, and ultrasound diathermy. Microwave diathermy uses high-frequency microwaves to generate heat in tissues, promoting healing and pain relief. Shortwave diathermy employs radiofrequency currents to stimulate deep tissues, improving blood flow and reducing muscle spasms. Ultrasound diathermy utilizes sound waves to produce thermal and non-thermal effects, aiding in tissue repair. Each type serves various therapeutic applications in physical therapy and rehabilitation settings, addressing specific patient needs.</p></p>
