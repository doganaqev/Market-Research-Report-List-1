<p><ul><li>Ductless Fume Hoods</li><li>Ducted Fume Hoods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laboratory fume hoods are essential safety devices used to handle hazardous substances. The market includes two main types: ductless fume hoods and ducted fume hoods. Ductless fume hoods are designed to filter and recirculate air within the lab, making them suitable for smaller spaces or where ducting is impractical. In contrast, ducted fume hoods vent toxic fumes directly outside, providing robust protection for users and the environment, and are typically used in larger or more hazardous laboratory settings.</p></p>
