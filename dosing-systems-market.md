<p><strong>The Dosing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Diaphragm</li><li>Piston</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dosing systems are crucial for precise fluid handling in various industries, primarily using diaphragm and piston types. Diaphragm dosing systems utilize a flexible membrane to create a seal, ensuring accurate and leak-free dosing of chemicals, making them ideal for corrosive or sensitive applications. In contrast, piston dosing systems employ a moving piston to draw and dispense fluids, providing high pressure and flow control, suitable for thicker or more viscous materials. Both types offer reliability and efficiency tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
