<p><p>The oral immunostimulant market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Interleukins** - proteins that mediate communication between immune cells, enhancing immune responses.</p><p>2. **Glucan** - natural polysaccharides that stimulate immune function and promote defense against pathogens.</p><p>3. **Lipopolysaccharides** - components of bacterial cell walls that activate immune responses, useful in vaccine development.</p><p>4. **Levamisole** - an anti-helminthic drug that also boosts immune activity.</p><p>5. **Isoprinosine** - a synthetic compound that stimulates immune function and is often used to treat viral infections.</p></p>
