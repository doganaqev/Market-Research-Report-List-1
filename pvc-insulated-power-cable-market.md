<p><p>The PVC insulated power cable market is categorized by conductor configuration: single strand, double strands, and multiple strands. Single strand cables consist of a single conductor, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for basic applications. Double strand cables feature two conductors, enhancing flexibility and capacity for power transmission. Multiple strand cables consist of several intertwined conductors, providing greater flexibility, durability, and resistance to fatigue, making them suitable for complex installations. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance and flexibility requirements.</p></p>
