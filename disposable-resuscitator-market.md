<p><strong>The Disposable Resuscitator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The disposable resuscitator market comprises self-inflating and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators are compact, requiring no external gas source, automatically inflating with each compression, making them ideal for emergency settings. Flow-inflating resuscitators, on the other hand, rely on a continuous gas flow, allowing for controlled ventilation and higher oxygen delivery, usually utilized in hospital environments. Both types serve critical roles in providing effective respiratory support during emergencies, catering to varying clinical needs and situations.</p></p>
