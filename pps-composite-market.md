<p><ul><li>Cross-linked Type PPS Composite</li><li>Linear Type PPS Composite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PPS composite materials are categorized into two main types: cross-linked and linear. Cross-linked PPS composites feature a network of interconnected polymer molecules, enhancing their thermal stability and mechanical strength, making them suitable for demanding applications. In contrast, linear PPS composites consist of unbranched polymer chains, offering flexibility and ease of processing. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, electronics, and aerospace, providing tailored properties like resistance to chemicals, heat, and wear, which are critical for performance in various environments.</p></p>
