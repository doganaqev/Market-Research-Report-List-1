<p><strong>The Polyester Strapping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embossed PET Strapping</li><li>Smooth PET Strapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyester strapping market is primarily divided into two types: embossed PET strapping and smooth PET strapping. Embossed PET strapping features a textured surface, enhancing grip and providing better friction, making it ideal for heavy or rough products. In contrast, smooth PET strapping has a sleek finish, which allows for easier feeding through machines and reduces wear on equipment. Both types offer superior tensile strength and resistance to environmental factors, making them suitable for various industrial applications, including packaging and shipping.</p></p>
