<p><p>Vertical filter market types in medical imaging technology are categorized based on detectors like Flat Panel Detectors (FPDs) and Computed Radiography (CR) Detectors. FPDs utilize a direct digital imaging process, providing high-resolution images and faster processing times, making them ideal for modern healthcare settings. Both types serve unique needs, influencing the selection based on factors like budget, image quality, and workflow efficiency.</p></p>
