<p><strong>The Radio Frequency Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive</li><li>Active</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radio Frequency Sensors can be categorized into two main types: passive and active. Passive sensors detect and respond to external radio frequency signals without generating their own, making them energy-efficient and ideal for applications like RFID and security systems. In contrast, active sensors emit their own RF signals to measure and monitor various parameters, providing more precise data. These devices find applications in industrial automation, automotive systems, and environmental monitoring, each serving distinct functional needs within the radio frequency landscape.</p></p>
