<p><ul><li>Cold Chain Transport</li><li>Non-cold Chain Transport</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biopharmaceutical transport market is divided into two main types: cold chain transport and non-cold chain transport. Cold chain transport is essential for temperature-sensitive biopharmaceuticals, requiring controlled environments to maintain efficacy, often involving refrigerated containers and specialized logistics. In contrast, non-cold chain transport deals with products that are stable at ambient temperatures, allowing for more flexible handling and distribution. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safe and effective delivery of biopharmaceutical products to healthcare providers.</p></p>
