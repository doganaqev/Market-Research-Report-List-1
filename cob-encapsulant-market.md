<p><p>The COB ( chip-on-board) encapsulant market primarily consists of two types: silicone and epoxy. Silicone encapsulants are known for their flexibility, high thermal stability, and excellent electrical insulation, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including automotive and consumer electronics. In contrast, epoxy encapsulants offer superior adhesion and mechanical strength, making them ideal for high-performance applications where durability and resistance to environmental factors are critical. Both types play crucial roles in protecting and enhancing the performance of electronic components.</p></p>
